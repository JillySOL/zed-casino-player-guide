# Jockeys and Trainers

Every horse is paired with a jockey and a trainer. They have names, portraits, and short bios. Each carries a hidden modifier profile:

* Jockey — affects in-race execution (e.g. better acceleration, better wet-condition handling).
* Trainer — affects long-term conditioning (e.g. better on specific surfaces or distances).

For Season One:

* Pairings are fixed. You can't swap, rotate, or acquire new jockeys/trainers.
* Modifier magnitudes are hidden, like DNA — learn through racing.
* Jockey/trainer swapping or a marketplace may come in future seasons.
