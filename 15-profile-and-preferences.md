# Profile and Preferences

The Profile page lets you manage:

| Setting | What It Does |
| --- | --- |
| Display name | The name shown on leaderboards (1–64 characters) |
| Odds format | Decimal or American (see [Reading a Race Card §4.3](04-reading-a-race-card.md)) |
| Stable customisation | Name, crest, colours (also available from My Stable) |
| Balance | Read-only — managed by your partner casino |

**Note.** Account-level settings (email, password, KYC) live in your partner casino, not in ZED. ZED uses your casino identity automatically.
