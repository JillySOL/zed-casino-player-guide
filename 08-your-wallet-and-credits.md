# Your Wallet and Credits

## Credits

All values in ZED are shown in credits, abbreviated cr. One credit equals one USD-equivalent unit of whatever currency your partner casino uses.

## Topping Up

You don't deposit into ZED directly. Your credits come from your partner casino account. To top up:

1. Leave the ZED iframe (or open your casino in a new tab).
2. Deposit through your casino's normal cashier flow.
3. Return to ZED — your new balance appears automatically.

## Withdrawing

The same applies to withdrawing. Cash out through your casino's cashier; ZED reflects the new balance.

## Why Crypto Price Movements Don't Bite You Mid-Bet

If your casino settles in a volatile crypto, ZED uses a currency-fluctuation policy so price movement between bet placement and settlement is predictable. The exact policy is documented in your casino's terms — look there for the specifics.
