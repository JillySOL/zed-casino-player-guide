# Bet History and P&L

## Where to Find It

Open the Bet History tab (or My Bets) from the main navigation.

## What You Can Do

* Filter by status: All / Won / Lost / Cancelled / Active.
* Filter by date range.
* Paginate through your full lifetime history.
* See per-bet detail: race, horse, stake, odds, return, status.

## P&L Summary

At the top of Bet History you'll see your lifetime stats:

| Stat | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Total Bets | Count of all bets placed |
| Total Wagered | Sum of all stakes |
| Total Returned | Sum of all winnings |
| Net P&L | Returned − Wagered |
| Win Rate | Won bets ÷ settled bets |

All in USD-equivalent credits.
