# Results and Payouts

## Settlement

Settlement is automatic and immediate:

* Winning bets credit your balance the moment the race finishes.
* Losing bets are simply lost (standard casino behaviour).
* Your Bet History is updated with the final status of every bet (Won / Lost / Cancelled / Pending).

## How Returns Are Calculated

For single bets, your return is stake × odds. Your profit is return − stake.

| Example | Stake | Odds | Return | Profit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Win at 4.50 | 10 cr | 4.50 | 45 cr | 35 cr |
| Place at 1.80 | 20 cr | 1.80 | 36 cr | 16 cr |
| Lost | 10 cr | — | 0 cr | −10 cr |

## House Edge

ZED applies a house margin to all bets. The headline figure is 10% for Win bets. This is built into the displayed odds — what you see is what you'd be paid, so you don't need to do extra maths.
